Project Objective

Build a 5-DOF Bipedal robot that implements state estimation (via IMU and contact sensors) and feedback control to dynamically balance and walk. Based on this paper by Disney https://la.disneyresearch.com/wp-content/uploads/BD_X_paper.pdf

Summary

MiniBot-V2 is a fully 3D printed bipedal robot powered by a raspberry pi-zero (which I plan to upgrade soon). Mini Bot uses a trained walking policy and feedback from IMU unit/Contact Sensors to dynamically balance and walk. I started this project as a platform to study/pratice sensor implentation and RL simulation (more on that on my MujoCo project page). I am currently working on a variety of mechanical improvements, including a redesign of the rechargable battery pack for increased capacity, a compute mounting redesign for lowered COG, and a redesign of the neck and joint linkages for reduced backlash.

Special thanks to Antoine Pirrone and Jaime Machucha for making this project possible, and for helping me debug my IMU.
